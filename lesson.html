Lesson: Croatian Daily Words

Welcome to the lesson! Here are some common Croatian words you'll be quizzed on:

  • Bok - Hello
  • Hvala - Thank you
  • Molim - Please
  • Doviđenja - Goodbye
  • Da - Yes
  • Ne - No
  • Oprosti - Excuse me/Sorry
  • Dobro jutro - Good morning
  • Laku noć - Good night
  • Voda - Water
  • Hrana - Food
  • Kava - Coffee
  • Čaj - Tea
  • Vino - Wine
  • Pivo - Beer
  • Jabuka - Apple
  • Naranča - Orange
